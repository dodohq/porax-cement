Company Profile

Porax Cement Ltd is the exclusive exporter of Vietnamese cement in the Southeast Asian region. With years of experience and network with companies with multiple production lines and plants, Porax Cement Ltd has an effective distribution network in the international market.

We are committed to providing high quality and consistent material for our clients to make their projects a success.

Products and Services

  • Portland Blended Cement - suitable for civil construction for infrastructure and industry projects. PCB 30 has stable compressive strength and better workability, and is able to provide smooth and fine surface as well as low water absorption to meet the market's demand. PCB 40 has higher quality and compressive strength. It is consistent and suitable for all civil construction work and projects, especially those that require high strength concrete for early strength development.
  • Bulk Cement - specially produced specialised concrete with high quality. High early strength development and high compressive strength. Bulk PCB 40 cement has low water demand, allowing for more a homogeneous mix. This high slump concrete allows for easy transportation and pumping. It has increased resistance for water absorption as well as erosion.
  • Ordinary Portland Cement - Stable properties and is suitable for high-quality construction projects. OPC has consistent high strength performance and excellent durability, resisting environmental corosion due to very low water permeability. Superior workability and relatively low water demand result in reduced concrete surface cracking. Enhanced cohesion with uniform concrete performance.
